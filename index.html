Laura Lara-Castor

Nutritional epidemiologist and data scientist specializing in global cardiometabolic disease, sugar-sweetened beverages, and comparative risk assessment. My work focuses on large-scale modeling and policy-relevant research using the Global Burden of Disease framework.

Research Focus

  • Global burden of cardiovascular disease
  • Dietary risk factors and chronic disease
  • Comparative risk assessment
  • Large-scale meta-analysis
